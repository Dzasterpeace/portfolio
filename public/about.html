About Alex Johnson

Welcome to My World of Design!

Hi there! I’m Alex Johnson, a passionate freelance graphic designer based in Seattle, Washington. For over a decade, I've been bringing concepts to life through innovative design solutions. Welcome to my corner of the internet where creativity meets functionality.

My Journey

I was always the kid who doodled in every notebook and dreamed in vivid colors. My journey into graphic design started at the University of Washington, where I earned my Bachelor's degree in Visual Communication Design. Post-graduation, I dove into the freelance world and haven't looked back since.

What I Do

  • Branding and Identity: Crafting unique brand images that resonate and endure.
  • Digital Illustration: Bringing ideas to life with visually captivating illustrations.
  • Web Design: Creating user-friendly, responsive, and visually appealing websites.
  • Print Design: From business cards to billboards, designing with an eye for detail and a flair for creativity.

My Philosophy

I believe in design that tells a story. Each project is an adventure, and my role is to weave your vision into a visual narrative that captivates and communicates. Good design is not just about looking great; it's about creating an experience and sparking a connection.
